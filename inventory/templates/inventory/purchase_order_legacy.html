📖 How to Read This Report

📊

Current Stock & Avg Daily

Live stock quantity from inventory. Avg/day is calculated from Tally TAX INVOICE data starting April 2025 — total qty sold ÷ days since April 1, 2025. This is the most reliable consumption figure.

📈

Growth (1-Year YoY & 3M vs Prev 3M)

1-Year: total Tally sales last 12 months vs previous 12 months. 3M: last 3 months vs 3 months before (Tally data). Blended monthly growth rate used in forecasting.

🔮

Sales Forecast (M+1 / M+2 / M+3)

Monthly growth rate (blended from 1Y + 3M) applied compoundingly to avg daily sales. M+1 = avg × (1+rate), M+2 = avg × (1+rate)², M+3 = avg × (1+rate)³. Shown only for active products.

💀

Dead Stock

No TAX INVOICE sale in the last 90 days. Dead items are excluded from ordering logic. Use "Hide Dead Stock" button to remove them from view.

👥

Top Customers

Click "View Top 5" to see the top 5 customers by total quantity purchased for that product, with a month-by-month breakdown. Loaded on demand via API.

🛒

Last Purchase Order & GST Purchases

PO: PURCHASE ORDER vouchers we made (from Dec 2024). GST Purchases: received and booked stock. Click "All X POs/receipts" for full history modal with date and qty.

📬

Order Recommendation Algorithm

1. Simulate daily stock drawdown with growth-adjusted consumption for 180 days.
2. Find the day stock hits zero → runway.
3. Reorder point = runway − lead time. If ≤ 0 → order immediately.
4. Qty = 3-month growth-adjusted forecast × 1.20 buffer − stock on arrival.
5. Round up to MOQ if needed. Click Show working for the full graph.

📦

Stock Runway

Current stock ÷ (avg/day × 30) = months until stock runs out (flat avg, conservative estimate). Overstocked = 9+ months.
